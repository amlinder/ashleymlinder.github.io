Artist Statement

I am an interdisciplinary artist who mainly focuses in Digital Media. Using digital means, I create artworks that aim to make an emotional impact on the viewer. These artworks range from video art, sound art, and digital composites, and can either be projected into large-scale pieces as well as on personal screens. While the message behind each piece varies, my main aim is to create work that conveys a specific emotion that the viewer can easily relate to or that they themselves have also experienced that same or similar emotion.
